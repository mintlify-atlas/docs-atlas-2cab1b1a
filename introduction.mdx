Svix makes it easy to send webhooks reliably at scale. You make one API call, and Svix takes care of delivery, automatic retries, signature verification, and more — across 9 official language SDKs.

Whether you're building a SaaS product that needs to notify customers of events, or consuming webhooks from third-party services, Svix has you covered.

## How Svix works

Svix is organized around a few core concepts:

- **Applications** — represent your customers or tenants. Each application has its own set of endpoints and message history.
- **Event Types** — define the schema and shape of events your platform produces.
- **Messages** — the individual webhook payloads you send to your customers.
- **Endpoints** — the URLs your customers register to receive webhook deliveries.
